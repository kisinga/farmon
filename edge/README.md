# AI Foundations for Business Leaders
## Private Training Program

### Program Overview

A structured 30-hour private training program designed for business owners and executives who want to leverage AI tools for increased productivity. No technical background required.

**Format:** 20 sessions × 1.5 hours  
**Duration:** 4 weeks (5 sessions/week)  
**Delivery:** 1-on-1, in-person or virtual  

---

## Who This Is For

- Business owners and executives
- Professionals managing operations, staff, or properties
- Those with basic computer proficiency seeking practical AI skills
- Leaders who want to understand AI before rolling it out to teams

---

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of this program, you will:

1. Understand what AI can and cannot do — cutting through the hype
2. Use AI assistants (ChatGPT, Claude) confidently for daily tasks
3. Draft communications, analyze documents, and research efficiently
4. Apply AI to your specific industry and workflows
5. Evaluate new AI tools independently
6. Understand risks, limitations, and responsible use

---

## Curriculum

### Module 1: Foundations (Week 1)
*Building understanding and first wins*

| Session | Topic | Description |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | What Is AI, Really? | Demystifying artificial intelligence. Mental models for understanding how AI tools work. What they can and cannot do. |
| 2 | First Conversations | Setting up accounts. Navigating the interface. Your first AI interactions. |
| 3 | The Art of Clear Instructions | How to communicate effectively with AI. Structure, context, and specificity. |
| 4 | Writing Assistance | Drafting emails, memos, and professional communications with AI support. |
| 5 | Practice & Review | Consolidation exercises. Q&A. Troubleshooting common issues. |

**Deliverable:** Personal prompt template library (5+ templates)

## Flex Options

### Condensed Program (20 hours) ▼
*For faster learners or narrower focus*

- Reduce practice/buffer sessions
- Focus on single industry vertical
- Skip advanced topics (voice, images)
- 3 weeks instead of 4

**Recommended if:** You learn quickly, have specific urgent needs, or want to test fit before committing to full program.
